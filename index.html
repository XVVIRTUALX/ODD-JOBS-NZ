JobsNZ

A modular workforce platform connecting people already living in New Zealand with practical job opportunities, sector-specific tools, and community-based work.

One Platform. Multiple Workforce Solutions.

JobsNZ is a modular platform hosting multiple job and workforce applications, beginning with Odd Jobs NZ and Immediate Painters. Each application operates independently while sharing a common platform vision.

Odd Jobs NZ

A community-based job-matching service connecting individuals with short-term, casual, and task-based work across New Zealand.
